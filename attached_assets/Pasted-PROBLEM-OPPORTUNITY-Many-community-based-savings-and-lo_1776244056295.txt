PROBLEM/OPPORTUNITY
Many community-based savings and loan groups face challenges such as:
-Manual and error-prone financial tracking
-Lack of transparency in loan and savings records
-Difficulty in member identification and data retrieval
-Limited access to real-time financial updates

Our digital solution addresses these challenges, providing an

automated system that ensures accuracy, security, and efficiency in

VSLA management

VALUE PROPOSITION

The INCLUDE US VSLA System is an innovative, digital solution designed to
enhance the efficiency and transparency of Village Savings and Loan
Associations (VSLA).
This automated system enables VSLA groups to manage savings, loans, and
financial records seamlessly. It allow each group to operate independently
while being managed under a centralized administration.
With real-time financial tracking, an intuitive user dashboard, and automatic
notifications for updates, the INCLUDE US VSLA System simplifies the
management of community savings and loan groups, empowering members
with financial insights and promoting digital financial inclusion.

KEY FEATURES

1: Member Management: Each member has a profile with a profile photo for
easy identification.
2: Savings & Loan Tracking: Members can view their personal savings, total
group savings, loan balance, and total group loan disbursed.
3:Loan Calculator: Admins, treasurers, and members can use the loan
calculator to determine loan repayment amounts, calculate interest, and track
the remaining loan balance.
4: Automated Reports: The system generates real-time financial reports for
transparency depending on the logged user role :
- Admin & Treasurer: Can add, edit, and manage savings and loan data.
- Members: Can view their personal financial status.
- Multisite Functionality: Each VSLA group operates as an independent
subsite under the main platform.
5: Secure Login & Registration: Ensures only authorized members access their
financial data.
6: Real-Time Loan Payment Tracking: The system tracks loan payments,
updates the remaining balance, and sends notifications when a payment is
made.
7: Notifications appear on the dashboard when savings or loan data is
updated, ensuring all members are aware of changes.
